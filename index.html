Collaboration & Cross-Posting
Collab posts and cross-posting are two of the highest-leverage tools on Instagram and Facebook. Done correctly, they double your reach without doubling your workload.
⚡
Instagram Collab Posts
1
Create your post or Reel as normal in Instagram
2
On the "Tag people" screen, tap Invite Collaborator
3
Search and select the collaborator account (vendor, influencer, partner brand)
4
Post — collaborator receives an invitation to accept
5
Once accepted, post appears on both feeds with both profile photos shown
Use for:
  • Vendor partnerships (Allergan, Galderma, SkinMedica)
  • Influencer gifted treatment posts
  • Local business cross-promotions
  • Providers with their own accounts
  • AMP corporate account amplification
🔄
Cross-Posting IG → Facebook
1
Instagram Settings → Account Center → Sharing Across Profiles
2
Connect your Facebook Page to your Instagram account
3
Toggle on "Share to Facebook" — set as default or choose per post
4
Reels, carousels, and feed posts all cross-post automatically
5
Stories can also be shared — toggle on per story before posting
Best practices:
  • Always cross-post Reels — Facebook Reels reach a different demographic
  • Before/afters and carousels perform well on both platforms
  • Facebook-only posts (events, boosted) still need to be created in Meta Business Suite
🤳
Influencer Collaboration
1
Identify local beauty/lifestyle influencers 3K–100K followers in your market
2
Send the Avelure Influencer Agreement before scheduling
3
Agree on deliverables: posts, Stories, Reels, timeline
4
Influencer must disclose "gifted" or use Instagram paid partnership label (FTC)
5
Request Collaborator invite on their post so it appears on Avelure feed too
FTC compliance:
  • All gifted/paid content must disclose "gifted," "ad," or paid partnership label
  • "Results may vary" must be included in all treatment result posts
  • No fabricated reviews or fake testimonials — ever
📤
Reposting & UGC
1
Monitor tagged posts and mentions daily in Instagram notifications
2
DM the patient: "We love your post! May we reshare on our Stories?"
3
Once confirmed — reshare to Stories via the paper airplane icon. Always tag the creator.
4
For Feed reposts: get written permission first. Always credit the original.
5
Save strong UGC to the Reviews highlight on the profile
Encourage UGC by:
  • Creating an Instagram-worthy photo area in the clinic
  • Asking patients to tag @aveluremedspa after treatment
  • "Tag us for a chance to be featured!" as a standing CTA in Stories
Content Collection — What to Request from Clinics
⚠ Patient Consent — Required Before Any Content Is Used
Every patient must sign a social media consent form before any photo or video is uploaded or used. No exceptions.
  • Keep a printed or digital consent form at the front desk
  • Patient signs before or immediately after treatment
  • Form must state photos/videos may be used on social media and marketing materials
  • Scanned forms go in the Consent Forms subfolder in the clinic drive
Need a consent form template? Contact the marketing team.

Photo & Video Standards
📷
Before & after photo requirements (per AMP SOP)
✓ Required
  • Neutral black or white backdrop
  • Patient standing with floor marker
  • Photographer floor marker for consistent distance
  • Ring light — never flash
  • All makeup removed
  • Face free of hair, glasses, headbands, jewelry
  • Angles: front, 45-degree, 90-degree left and right
  • Submit raw full images — no cropping
  • Include: practice, provider, treatment, product used, amount, time between photos, restrictions
✗ Never do
  • Do not edit photos or apply filters
  • Do not create side-by-side templates — raw only
  • Do not use flash
  • Do not take "after" immediately post-treatment — wait 2 weeks minimum
  • Do not submit without signed consent form
  • Before & Afters: organic posts ONLY — NOT allowed in paid Meta ads
🎥
Video shooting standards
✓ Required
  • Film vertically (portrait) — always
  • Stabilize on a surface or tripod
  • Bright room — light facing the subject
  • 15–60 second clips
  • Quiet room — no background music
  • Send original unedited file — no filters, text, or overlays
  • Reels: custom cover image in Canva, captions ON, hook in first 2 seconds
✗ Never do
  • Don't film horizontally (landscape mode)
  • Don't film with light behind the subject
  • Don't include other patients in background
  • Don't add music, text, or stickers — send raw
  • Don't film in dark or yellow-lit rooms
  • Don't send over email — upload to folder directly
